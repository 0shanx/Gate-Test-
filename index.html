The PYQ Imperative: Why Past Papers Matter

Practicing with Previous Year Questions (PYQs) in a timed, test-like environment is no longer just a trend—it's a cornerstone of modern GATE preparation. It hones exam temperament, builds time management skills, and familiarizes aspirants with the exact patterns and question styles of the actual exam.
